# Paintmates — Business Overview

## About the Business

**Paintmates** is a professional painting company based in **Brisbane, Queensland, Australia**. The business specialises in residential and commercial painting services across the greater Brisbane metropolitan area, Logan City, and surrounding South-East Queensland regions.

Paintmates was built on a simple philosophy: every customer deserves the same high standard of workmanship, care and respect — regardless of job size. From a single ceiling touch-up to a full commercial fitout, every project is approached with the same professionalism and attention to detail.

## Services Offered

### 1. Interior Painting
Transform the inside of any home or business. Covers walls, ceilings, trims, skirtings, doors, feature walls and more. Clean precise lines and a flawless finish that lasts for years.

### 2. Exterior Painting
Protect Brisbane homes from the harsh Queensland sun, rain and humidity with professional exterior repaints using weather-resistant, UV-stable coatings. Boosts property value and kerb appeal.

### 3. Roof Painting
Restore faded roofs with high-pressure cleaning, anti-fungal primer and premium roof coatings. Dramatically improves home appearance and extends roof life.

### 4. Fence & Deck Painting
Specialising in staining, sealing, oiling and painting fences and decks in Queensland's demanding climate. Protects against UV, moisture and everyday wear.

### 5. Commercial Painting
Professional painting for offices, retail spaces, warehouses, strata complexes and body corporates. After-hours availability to minimise disruption to businesses.

### 6. New Builds & Renovations
Works alongside builders and renovators to deliver immaculate interior and exterior finishes for new construction and renovation projects. Understands builder timelines.

## What Makes Paintmates Different

### Thorough Surface Preparation
Every surface is sanded, filled, primed and prepped before a single drop of paint is applied. This is what separates a 2-year finish from a 10-year finish.

### Premium Paint Products Only
Uses only top-grade **Dulux**, **Taubmans** and **Haymes** paints — the best products available in Australia for durability and finish quality.

### Multiple Coats, Always
Every job receives the correct number of coats for a rich, even, professional finish. Single-coat shortcuts are never taken.

### Fully Insured
Carries full public liability insurance. ABN registered.

### On Time, Every Time
Arrives exactly when scheduled. No last-minute cancellations or no-shows.

### Clean & Respectful
All furniture and flooring are protected with drop sheets and plastic sheeting. The site is fully cleaned up before leaving.
